The philosophy

Raw edges become
rare finds

Sea glass starts as something broken — discarded, sharp, ordinary. The ocean takes over: grinding it against sand and rock, turning it in the current, smoothing every jagged edge over years of patient work. What washes ashore is something transformed, worth finding, worth keeping.

Your infrastructure challenge is no different. A legacy codebase sprawling across 200 microservices. A CI/CD pipeline held together by tribal knowledge. An LLM adoption initiative without guardrails. These aren't failures — they're raw material.

SeaGlassPro brings 20+ years of tidal pressure across fintech, healthcare, media, and SaaS to turn your rough spots into polished, production-ready solutions. The ocean doesn't give up on the glass. Neither do I.

The process

How the ocean works — and how I work

01 ☁

Washed Ashore

Every engagement starts with discovery. I dive into your stack, your team's pain points, your deployment cadence. No assumptions — just listening. Like glass arriving on the beach, every situation is unique.

02 ⚙

Into the Tumble

Terraform. Kubernetes. Github/GitLab. Python. Java. Erlang. .... The tools are the tide — constant, relentless, precise. I apply IaC improvements, streamline pipelines, and start reducing that version sprawl. The tumbling begins.

03 ◈

Taking Shape

Patterns emerge. Golden paths solidify. Whether it's collapsing 30 language versions to 4 or designing LLM governance guardrails, the rough edges start to smooth into something recognizable and reliable.

04 ✦

Polished & Kept

The result is infrastructure worth keeping. Documented, transferable, resilient. Your team owns it. The ocean's work is done — and what's left is something genuinely rare: a system you're proud of.
